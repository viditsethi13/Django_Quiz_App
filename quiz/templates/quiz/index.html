Quiz Me Up

Quiz Me Up(also simply known as QMU) is a Database System Quizzing Platform. It is a MCQ based approach to test your knowledge of Database System. In this quiz, user gets 5 Random questions from the pool of about 50 questions. Test terminates when user gives a wrong answer or user answers all 5 questions. Platform lets you tracks your performance. It shows your last 2 quiz score. Quiz yourself to check your grasp on Database System
